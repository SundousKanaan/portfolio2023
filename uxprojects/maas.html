MAAS

A UX Designer is responsible for the comprehensive development of a product, encompassing design, usability, functionality, branding, and marketing. Their work spans the entire user journey, from initial interaction to final stages, and involves identifying new opportunities for both the product and the company.

The assignment

The mission was to enhance the user experience of MAAS coffee machines through comprehensive research on the machines, as well as different coffee or tea moments and alternatives. The outcome was a final poster highlighting the key findings from the research.

Research results

After some user testing, watching interviews and doing some research, I found ten solutions to our user problems and made lo-fi sketches for that.

End improve advice

In the end I chose to make my solution advice work with the machine system and explained the steps of the solution by designing a sequential design of the machine screen with the addition of adjustments.
Below is a screen where the user can determine how much sugar, coffee and milk is in his drink. There is also an indicator for calories and also two buttons to choose between coffee beans and powder. Of course everything works with touch.

End poster

This poster contains the research results, the first solutions and the chosen advice with clear explanations and designs.
