Young Artis Experience

Artis Park in Amsterdam gave us a mission to design an experience for its young visitors so that they can wander around it without relying on adults and enjoy a unique experience during their visit.

Project plan

I created a comprehensive project plan to ensure a clear understanding between myself and the client. It includes a debriefing, insights, focus, design direction, research methods, and visual style results. This allows me to demonstrate my understanding of the assignment and address any issues before beginning the design process.

My Concepts

I generated three concept ideas for my work, received feedback from my client, and selected the best one to further enhance. For each idea, I developed a concept, timeline, and a brief storyboard to provide a clear understanding. Ultimately, I chose my second idea, making improvements and implementing a few changes.

Chosen concept

Maru, the clever friend.
Maru is a device with a smart system. He can remember the sound and name of the child to communicate by his own name and respond according to the sound of the child. Everything will also happen through sound.
He can also:

  • Provide assistance with navigation.

  • Take photos and have album with options.

  • Help the child if he gets lost in the crowds.

  • Playing with the child and giving fun assignments.

  • Provide information about the animals and answer the child's questions.
